Who We Are

Tommy Talks provides an online platform for talented and innovative students, faculty and alumni to spread their ideas in the form of short, powerful talks.

Mission

Our mission is to share ideas, inspire students, and connect communities within University of Southern California.

Share

Tommy Talks highlights students, faculty, and alumni who have thought-provoking ideas. Our content consists, but is not limited to, of motivational speeches, social issue debates, student-to-student teaching series, faculty advice, and creative projects on campus.

Inspire

Tommy Talks encourages students to get involved, discover like-minded peers, and create their own messages. By showcasing these ideas, we foster a community of creativity, innovation, passion, and curiosity.

Connect

Tommy Talks opens the dialogue between students on campus. We connect those who otherwise would not meet. We create a space where the student population is aware and motivated so that they can positively influence the larger community.
